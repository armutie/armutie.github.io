UW BattleBot

150g vertical spinner

Overview

I teamed up with three classmates to design and build a 150g combat robot for UW BattleBots. I owned mechanical design: CAD, 3D printing, and weapon fabrication.

Out of 20+ teams, only 7 finished a working bot. Ours survived 3 rounds.

Evolution

My favorite image; the design went through multiple iterations as we refined the chassis, weapon mounting, and electronics layout.

The Weapon

The vertical spinner was machined from a single aluminum block for maximum strength.

In testing, it shredded through an aluminum can in seconds.
